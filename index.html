About Me

I bridge the gap between complex data and clear understanding. With a background in both advanced data analysis for global banks and developing training programs for Egypt's future pioneers, I don't just build dashboards—I build confidence.

I specialize in turning raw data into actionable insights and empowering teams to use them effectively. My unique combination of technical expertise and teaching experience allows me to communicate complex concepts clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences.
